About

In 1975, the US and the USSR worked together on the Apollo-Soyuz Test Project. A spare Apollo Command/Service Module and Soyuz 19 docked together in orbit to symbolize the end of the US-USSR space race. Pictured is Astronaut Thomas P. Stafford (top, US) and Cosmonaut Aleksei A. Leonov (bottom, USSR).
