About

Voyager (1 and 2) were sent out to study the farthest planets in 1977 and have now left the solar system. The card shows two images. One is a sketch for a Voyager probe, a lonely searcher that has been sent off farther than anything else. The background shows Earth, an isolated civilization among mostly empty space. Earth is a single dot in the faint orange line farthest to the right.
